About Me

I am Junior developer. I was born in Myanmar. I am currently living in Yangon. I'm 23 years old.
I studied civil engineering before the COVID-19 pandemic with the support of my parents. At that time, I didn't know what my dream was.
After COVID-19, I came to understand what I truly want to do. My ultimate goal is to become a successful IT professional, contributing to the industry while continuously growing and improving myself.
So in 2022, I started learning IT while also studying the Japanese language.
